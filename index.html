Welcome to Slovak Community Charity

We are a dedicated charity focused on supporting the Slovak community in Greater Manchester. Our aim is to provide educational resources, cultural activities, and community support to help Slovak-speaking families maintain their heritage while living abroad.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a vibrant community where Slovak culture and language thrive. We believe in fostering a sense of belonging and identity through education, cultural events, and community engagement.

Programs and Services

We offer a variety of programs and services to support our community:

  • Language Classes : Learn Slovak in a supportive and interactive environment.
  • Cultural Events : Celebrate Slovak traditions with music, dance, and food.
  • Community Support : Connect with other Slovak-speaking families and individuals.
  • Educational Workshops : Enhance your skills and knowledge through our workshops.

Get Involved

There are many ways to get involved with our charity:

  • Volunteer : Help us with events and activities.
  • Donate : Support our programs and services.
  • Join : Become a member and participate in our activities.
  • Spread the Word : Share our mission with your friends and family.
