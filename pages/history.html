History of Slovak Community Charity

Founding and Mission

The Slovak Community Charity, officially known as Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester, was founded with the mission to preserve and promote Slovak language and culture. Our journey began with a group of dedicated teachers who shared a common vision—to establish an educational and cultural centre that truly makes a difference.

Early Beginnings

Our charity was officially registered on 9th September 2024, marking a significant milestone in our journey. The official launch on 15th September 2024 was celebrated in collaboration with the Faculty of Education from Univerzita Mateja Bela and the Association of Slovak Schools and Community Centres in the UK.

Growth and Development

Since our inception, we have focused on providing Slovak language education and organizing cultural and community activities. Our Saturday school offers structured Slovak language lessons for children and adults, along with various workshops, events, and social activities. These initiatives help Slovak-speaking families maintain their heritage while living abroad.

Key Milestones

  • • 2024 : Registered as a charity and launched officially.
  • • 2024 : Established partnerships with Slovak educational institutions and community centres.
  • • 2025 : Expanded our programs to include healthcare support, cultural events, and community gatherings.

    • Cultural and Educational Impact

    Our charity has played a crucial role in bridging the gap between the UK and Slovak education systems. We ensure that children who may one day return to Slovakia can transition smoothly. Our programs include:

  • • Slovak Language Lessons : Engaging and structured classes for children and adults.
  • • Cultural Events & Workshops : Celebrating Slovak traditions through interactive activities.
  • • Community & Social Gatherings : Opportunities to meet, connect, and build lasting friendships.

    • Recent Developments

    In recent years, we have introduced new programs focused on mental health support, vocational training, and environmental sustainability. Our commitment to making a positive impact remains unwavering.

    Looking Ahead

    As we look to the future, we are excited to continue our work and expand our reach. Our vision is to create a thriving, inclusive community where everyone has the opportunity to succeed and contribute to society. We invite you to join us in this journey and support our mission.
